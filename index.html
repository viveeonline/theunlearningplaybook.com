The Core Belief
In the AI era, success is no longer just about learning what’s new — it’s about the insight and courage to abandon what’s outdated.

The strategies that got us promoted five years ago are the same ones holding our organisations back today. The hardest part of leadership isn’t knowing more. It’s letting go. That’s where The Unlearning Playbook begins.

About The Show

A podcast built on
one sharp thesis.

01
What It Is

A ~60-minute interview podcast where senior leaders candidly deconstruct their journey through the specific lens of unlearning and relearning.

02
Who It’s For

Senior leaders and ambitious professionals navigating disruption — especially those asking whether their experience is holding them back.

03
Why Now

AI is accelerating the obsolescence of old strategies faster than ever. The leaders winning today are those who unlearn fastest — not just learn fastest.

Every Episode — The Arc

Three phases.
One transformation.

01
Phase 01
The Old Playbook

The belief or strategy that defined the guest’s early success — and eventually became the very thing holding them back.

02
Phase 02
The Trigger

The pivotal failure, shift, or realization that forced them to question the rules they’d built their success on.

03
Phase 03
The New Playbook

The fresh principles, mindsets, and strategies they adopted — and the concrete steps they took to find success again.
